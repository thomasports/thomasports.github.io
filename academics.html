At St. Benedicts, I participated in the cross country, basketball, and volleyball programs. Those programs helped shape who I am today. They were the first sports I played and they are the only reason I am good at some sports to this day. They also helped me gain the studying and academic skills that I have today. Although I am not there now, it will always hold a special place in my life for the first amazing school I have ever gone to.
