Hobbies

Video Games

One of my favorite things to do when I get home from school is play video games. Sometimes I play with my friends, but sometimes I play by myself, because sometimes I feel better playing by myself. My favorite video games are Minecraft, Rocket League, and Fortnite. I usually play Rocket League and Fortnite with my friends, but I play Minecraft by myself because it is peaceful that way. Although it is not productive, playing video games is very fun and enjoyable.

Going to the gym

Sometimes after school, I like to hit the gym. I have a regular pattern of what muscles to work out. Currently, the pattern goes like this: Day 1 - Chest and Triceps, Day 2 - Back and Biceps, Day 3 - Legs and Shoulders, and Day 4 - Core. This pattern keeps my body in tact, while also working out all of the necessary muscles. After each workout, I pick between either doing plyometrics or cardio. I usually pick plyometrics, because it helps with volleyball, but I still do cardio a good amount. This workout routine helps me stay in shape.
